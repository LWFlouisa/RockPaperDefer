# Longterm Design Goals
* Limit usage of automated character to emergency situations, simulating fight or flight responces.
* Focus gameplay on provision distribution and scavenging.
* Gradually expand possible acts to include traditional cave man hunting.
* Comparisons of ancient temples to Yonagumi.

## Fight Or Flight
The Ai currently most simulates fight or flight responces, or an automated character when a player is close to game over.

## Focus On Nursing
This aims to focus on logistics and first aid provisioning rather than direct combat mechanics.

## Traditional Hunting
In an earlier version the Ruby form was compared to stealth games, but its more accurate to compare it to traditional hunting.

## Funeral Archeology
The combined practice of analysing burial customs and ancient temples like the Sunken Yonagumi.

# Current Gameplay
Current gameplay closely resembles Rock Paper Scissors, and similar hand game practices will carry over into stealth mechanics.
